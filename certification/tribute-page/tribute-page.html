Here's a time line of Ada Lovelace

  • 1815 - was born on December 10, 1815 in London(UK). Her birth name was Augusta Ada Byron.
  • 1820 - Ada showed a gift for mathematics at an early age. Her dad, Lord Byron who was a famous poet died when she was only 8 years old.
  • 1828 - when Ada was only twelve she made a small flying machine.
  • 1833 - at a party Ada meets Charles Babbage and was fascinated by his ideas for a machine that could calculate numbers and write letters. Ada was 17 when she met him.
  • 1835 - Ada married William King.
  • 1837 - Ada and Babbage tested the first computer program. And Ada wrote the first computer program.
  • 1838 - William king and Ada Lovelace became Earl and Countess of Lovelace.
  • 1842 - she translated an Italian mathematician's memoir in french in the subject of the Analytical Engine.
  • 1843 - Ada wrote a book about C, Ada published her work and used the initials A.A.L standing for Augusta Ada Lovelace.
  • 1852 - Lovelace died at the age of 36 on 27 November 1852, from uterine cancer.
